NUST Entry Test (NET) Complete Guide

Everything you need to score 160+ and secure admission to your dream program at NUST.

📋
NET Structure & Format
  • Computer-based test (CBT)
  • Total: 200 marks each NET type
  • No negative marking — attempt every question
  • 4 series in 2026 — best score used for merit
  • Results next day at 10:00 AM
  • Register at ugadmissions.nust.edu.pk
  • Test cities: Islamabad, Karachi, Quetta, Gilgit
📐
5 NET Types — Choose Yours
Engineering/CS Math 50% · Physics 30% · English 20%
Business/Social Quant Math 40% · English 30% · IQ+GK 30%
Applied Sciences Biology 40% · Chemistry 30% · English 15% · IQ 15%
Architecture Aptitude 50% · Math/Physics 30% · English 20%
Natural Sciences Mathematics 50% · English 50%
🎯
Preparation Strategy
  • Identify your NET type first — prepare accordingly
  • Math first — highest weightage in most NETs
  • Focus on FSc board syllabus only
  • Practice past papers from ugadmissions.nust.edu.pk
  • ~54 seconds per question — practice speed daily
  • No penalty — attempt every single question
  • Take mock tests under real timed conditions
  • Appear in all 4 series — NUST uses your best score
🔢
Merit Formula (Memorize This)
Aggregate =
 (NET / 200) × 75
+ (FSc / FSc_Total) × 15
+ (Mat / Mat_Total) × 10
  • Max aggregate = 100%
  • Each NET mark = +0.375% to aggregate
  • No bonus marks exist — aggregate is purely formula-based
  • SAT ≥ 1100 accepted instead of NET
